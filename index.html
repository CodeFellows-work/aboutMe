Hello! My name is Sunny and I was born and raised in Washington state. Although I have made mutliple attempts to leave, life always brought me back.

My First Attempt To Leave Washington State

My very first attempt to leave the state, began when I was given my first opportunity to leave the state - that was... turning 18. My ticket was the U.S. Navy. The Navy has brought me all sorts of opportunity, but leaving Washington was not one of them. After bootcamp and "A" school (a technical school) I was given a list of options to choose orders for. As I was at the top of my class, I was given the opportunity to choose first. Among the choices were:

  • Japan
  • Italy
  • California
  • More California
  • The boat (the most undesired)
  • Oak Harbor, Washington

I chose everything except for Oak Harbor, Washington. But life just said no. I eneded up being stationed in Oak Harbor, Washington. As I exited the service and remained in Washington, I realized the beauty of being home. I now love Washington.
